Section 01

Core Equation — What Must Be True

Before evaluating verticals, we define constraints based on SquadStack's Fundamental Equations.

The Unit Economics Stack
Profit_Lead = Revenue_Lead − Cost_Lead
Revenue_Lead = ( Talk_Minutes × Price/Min ) + ( Conversion_Prob × Success_Fee )
Cost_Lead = AI_Routing_Cost + ( Human_Talk_Min × Agent_Payout ) + Telecom_Cost
→ LTV/CAC must clear 8x (SquadStack's 10x growth mandate)
→ AI must handle ≥50% of talk minutes to protect gross margin
📈

Economic Ceiling

Volume × Margin

Is the Cost Per Acquisition (CAC) high enough that the client will pay a premium "Success Fee"? Are there enough leads to amortize campaign setup costs?

🤖

AI-Readiness

Script Complexity

Can the Voice AI handle top-of-funnel qualification entirely? Highly emotional or consultative sales score low; transactional filtering scores high.

⚡

Integration Velocity

CRM Maturity

Does the industry use modern, API-ready CRMs (Salesforce, HubSpot, LeadSquared), or legacy on-premise systems? Fast integration = fast Time-to-Revenue.

👥

Supply Friction

Staffing Complexity

Does the vertical require highly specialized, expensive human agents (hard to scale/replace), or can average agents close the deal using AI co-pilots?

Section 02

The Battle of the Verticals

Applying the rubric to three high-growth telecalling consumers in India.

EdTech

UpGrad, PhysicsWallah, Amity

The Reality

The Indian EdTech telecalling market handles massive volume but suffers from abysmal 2-3% conversion rates. Sales are highly consultative — "Joint Family Decisions."

SquadStack Fit

While volume is high, AI cannot close a ₹2 Lakh course. It requires high-tier human counselors (High Supply Friction). If an agent churns, retraining is a massive drag on margins.

Healthcare & Clinics

Diagnostic Chains, Mid-sized Clinics

The Reality

Mid-sized Indian clinics miss ~20% of inbound calls, leaking an estimated ₹7.4 Lakhs annually per clinic.

SquadStack Fit

AI-readiness is perfect (transactional appointment bookings). However, the market is highly fragmented. Selling to 100 clinics to equal the ACV of one enterprise deal creates a massive sales bottleneck.

🏆 The Winner

Godrej, Lodha, NoBroker

The Reality

Developers pay ₹1,000–₹2,500 for a raw digital lead (CPL). However, the actual Cost Per Site Visit (CPSV) skyrockets to ₹6,000–₹12,000 because agents are too slow to call leads (1-5% conversion). Speed-to-lead (calling within 5 mins) is the ultimate metric.

SquadStack Fit

The holy grail. AI can instantly dial the lead in 30 seconds, ask standard filtering questions (Budget? 2BHK/3BHK? Location?), and pass only verified, high-intent leads to human closers.

Section 04

The GTM Playbook for Real Estate

Capture the Real Estate market with the "5-Minute Site Visit Engine."

01

The Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

🎯

Target

VP of Sales / Chief Marketing Officer at Top 50 Indian Developers & Aggregators

" Stop paying ₹8,000 per site visit because your call center takes 2 hours to dial Facebook leads. Our AI stack dials in 30 seconds, filters out the junk, and our human experts book the site visits. "
02

The Pricing Architecture

A hybrid model to align incentives while protecting AI compute costs.

Base

Platform / Setup Fee

Covers initial CRM integration and agent training.

Usage

Per-Minute Rate

Minimal per-minute rate for AI talk-time (covers telephony/LLM COGS).

💰 Margin Driver

Bumper Success Fee

₹750 – ₹1,000 per verified Site Visit booked. Developers currently pay ₹6k–12k per visit — a massive win-win.

03

The 30-Day Enterprise Pilot

⚠ Do not risk live enterprise leads until the model is proven.

Week 1

Integration

Rapid CRM connection — LeadSquared / Salesforce. Set up data pipelines and lead routing rules.

Week 2

The "Dead Lead" Test

Client provides 2,000 leads marked as "Dead/Unresponsive" from the last 6 months. SquadStack unleashes purely Voice AI to reactivate them at near-zero cost. This builds trust.

Week 3–4

Live Routing

SquadStack intercepts live Facebook ad leads. AI handles the first 90 seconds (Intent, Budget, Timeline). Top 20% are warm-transferred to SquadStack's human real-estate experts to lock in the site visit.
